PHOTO TIPS: Best Practices

1 Load Up to (3) Photos

  • One full length photo: on a hanger or yourself with a frame of space.
  • One detailed shot: if there’s a logo, detail, or pattern, show it).
  • One price tag close up.

2 Lighting Matters

  • Select the auto setting on your device's camera

3 Style Matters

  • Avoid distracting backgrounds.
  • Avoid reflective surfaces (mirror, glass metal, etc.)

4 Location, Location, Location

  • Allow MySaleTable to use your phone’s GPS, so it’s easy to select the store where you're posting.

5 Good, Clean Shopping Fun

  • If a photo may offend, rethink your post.
